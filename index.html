01. Motivation & Research

The Why: Choosing the Active Track

Our team selected the Active Track because we identified a critical disconnect between the high-quality fitness facilities at XJTLU and the actual training efficiency of students. While many students are motivated to improve their physical health, they often face "analysis paralysis" due to scientific data opacity. We chose this track to transform the solitary and often confusing gym experience into a data-driven, scientific journey. FITGRID addresses the "Active" challenge by integrating Metabolic Equivalent (MET) calculations with real-time occupancy tracking, ensuring that physical activity is not just frequent, but purposeful and synchronized with personal physiological limits. By focusing on this track, we aim to leverage software engineering to solve the real-world problem of training plateaus and gym congestion, ultimately fostering a more sustainable and scientific fitness culture within our university community.

User Journey Map
Requirements List

3 must-haves for the system to be considered “Playful”

  • Visual Grid Calibration: Replaces traditional text inputs with an interactive 3x3 grid to make physiological goal-setting intuitive and engaging.
  • Live Occupancy Feedback: Utilizes dynamic pulses and color-coded scales to display real-time gym load, significantly increasing data transparency.
  • Adaptive Interface Theming: Switches UI color schemes dynamically based on user attributes to provide immediate, personalized visual confirmation.
